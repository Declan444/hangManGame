HangMan

Game Information

  • Click a letter
  • Click enter
  • If letter is in the word, it will be placed in the correct positon.
  • If the letter is incorrect the hangman will start. All selected letters will turn Red
  • If the letter is in the word, the hanging does not progress
  • When you know the word, press solve and enter the word. If correct, you win the game, if incorrect another hanging section is added.
